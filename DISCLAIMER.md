## Research and Educational Use Only

This wildlife conservation tools project is designed for **research and educational purposes only**. It is not intended for operational use in real-world wildlife conservation scenarios.

## Important Limitations

### Synthetic Data
- All data used in this demonstration is **synthetically generated**
- The ecological patterns, human activity, and threat levels are simulated
- Real conservation data would require different preprocessing, validation, and modeling approaches

### Model Limitations
- Models have not been validated with real wildlife conservation data
- Performance metrics are based on synthetic data and may not reflect real-world accuracy
- Model predictions should not be used to make actual conservation decisions

### Privacy and Security
- Real conservation data often contains sensitive location information
- Endangered species locations must be protected from public disclosure
- This demo uses synthetic coordinates that do not correspond to real conservation areas

## Ethical Considerations

### Wildlife Conservation
- Conservation decisions should always involve wildlife biology experts
- AI models should complement, not replace, human expertise and local knowledge
- Consider the potential impact on wildlife and local communities

### Data Privacy
- Real conservation data may contain personally identifiable information
- Location data should be anonymized or aggregated when possible
- Follow data protection regulations and conservation organization policies

### Bias and Fairness
- Models may reflect biases in training data
- Consider how predictions might affect different communities or species
- Regular model auditing and bias testing is essential
